About AntID Tutor

AntID Tutor is an educational platform that combines artificial intelligence and computer vision to help students identify ant genus and learn about myrmecology.

Our mission is to make ant identification and learning accessible to everyone, from curious beginners to advanced biology students and researchers.

Our Technology

Convolutional Neural Network

Our genus identification system uses a deep learning CNN model trained on thousands of ant images. The model analyzes visual patterns to identify ant genus with high accuracy.

GPT-based Tutor

AntTutor uses advanced natural language processing to provide detailed, educational responses to your questions about ants, their behavior, ecology, and more.

Interactive Learning

Dynamic quizzes and educational content help reinforce learning and test your knowledge of ant genus, behavior, and ecology.

Educational Purpose

AntID Tutor is designed for educational purposes to help students:

  • Learn to identify common ant genus
  • Understand ant morphology, behavior, and ecology
  • Explore the geographic distribution of different ant genus
  • Contribute to citizen science through observation reporting
  • Develop a deeper appreciation for these important insects

Disclaimer

While our identification system is highly accurate, it should be used for educational purposes only. For scientific research or pest management decisions, please consult with professional myrmecologists or entomologists.
